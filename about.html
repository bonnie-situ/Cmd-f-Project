About De sci fi

Who We Are

A dedicated group of first year UBC students hoping to contribute to a more accessible world. #forthegreatergood

What We Do

    Transcribe texts from poorly scanned or hard to read images or pdfs
    Deliver seamless results when DeciphAI is paired with a screenreader
    In the future, we hope to develop an app that can scan images instantaneously transcribe text.

Currently...

Many image to text scanner uses Terreract, an optical character recognition engine.

However, there are still programs with the engine with text from poorly scanned images or handwritten notes not being transcribed correctly

How will De sci fi work?

Help us train the program! We need samples of your hard to read or waterlogged writing samples!

  1. Take an image of something unreadable.
    Note: Make sure to save the writing sample as a pdf, jpg or png file.
  2. Upload your image to our form to contribute to our database!
    Note: Include your email if you wish to see the results or learn more about the development of our project!
